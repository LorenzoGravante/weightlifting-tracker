# training-tracker
Web-based app to log training sessions, calculate progression with science-backed techniques, and monitor PRs and progression. 
Available at https://lorenzogravante.github.io/training-tracker/
Open the link in your preferred mobile browser and add a shortcut to your phone's home page

Your training data lives in your browser's localStorage — it stays on the device/browser you use. If you switch phones or browsers, export your JSON backup from Settings and import it on the new device.
